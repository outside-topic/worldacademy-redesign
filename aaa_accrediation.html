AAA Accreditation

Our Commitment to Quality: AAA Accreditation We believe that trust is the foundation of professional development. That is why the World Academy for Research and Development has pursued and achieved accreditation from the American Accreditation Association (AAA) . Key Highlights of our Accreditation: The Standard: We are certified compliant with ISO/IEC 17024:2012 – the international benchmark for organizations offering personnel certification. The Scope: Our accreditation covers the field of Personnel Certification, validating the integrity of our examination and certification processes. The Verdict: This accreditation confirms that our graduates have been assessed against the strictest international criteria. Verification: You can verify our status using Certificate Number PC26305 on the official AAA database.
